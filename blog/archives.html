    The quick, white seagull flew over a lazy city last night in a stylish manner.

    The seagull seemed like he was hunting for food as he was actually pretty close to the ground but at least enough to clear trees. The lazy seagull then landed on the beach that was by a rocky ledge. The seagull was looking for food, roving around the sand like a scavenger on the prowl.

    The seagull did this when he begged for food:

    1. Made eye contact with a beach goer
    2. Opened his beak
    3. Made a loud caw sound
