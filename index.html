About Michael Krieger

Michael Krieger is the co-founder of Health Innovators. Health Innovators is a nonprofit organization that strives to improve the healthcare industry through collaboration and education. He also is a part of Find My Nurse, which is a virtual nurse service.

He enjoys being a part of the Nashua-Hudson Toastmasters, which he has been a part of for the past year and a half. He achieved the Competent Communicator Award. In his spare time, he likes to ski and referee soccer.
